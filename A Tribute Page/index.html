Mohamed Ibrahim Warsame (Hadraawi)

Biography about a poet Hadraawi

His name is Mohamed Ibrahim Warsame , known by the pseudonym (Hadrawi).

Born 1943 - died 18 August 2022.

he was a Somali poet, philosopher and songwriter.

some of his noteable work : Hoooyoy la'aantaa , sirta nolosha ,isa sudhan.

Hadrawi died in Hargeisa , Somaliland on 18 August 2022, at the age of 79.
